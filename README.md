# Bananalution!
Desccriptions:

Play through our platfomer, collecting BANANAS to MORPH in to different organisms, in a similar ORDER as to the what the earth had. Starting with FISH, work your way up to becoming the pinnacle of human evolution, a hack club member!

---
## How to Play (or see "how to play" tab in the game itself!)
---
For laptops
1. navigate to our site
2. use wasd or arrow keys to move
---
For Phones/Tablets
1. navtigate to the site
2. click on buttons or use joystick to move

---
With Bananalution not-even-official controller  
  
To setup
1. download gerbers and cad (to be added extremely soon)
2. print out case, and manufacture pcb somehow
3. solder arduino nano and joystick
4. upload arduino code (found in /hardware) to arduino nano
5. Plug it in to COM 13
6. run python script (RunOnHostDevice.py)

## Motivation:
We drew: Fish, Banana, order, morph

What better way to demonstrate these cards than through the evolution of organisms of the earth! Introducing Bananlution

Our project is a platformer game where you start as a fish. You collect bananas to morph into new organisms, like Blahaj and Orpheus, in the correct order. It’s just like the real world!

Different organisms will have different movement mechanics, such as swimming, swinging, crawling, and jumping.
